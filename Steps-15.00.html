Creating Visual Card 15


Adding Visual card for Sales Per MTh, %, Sales MoM%, Sales YoY% and Property Tax%

Steps

Sales per MTh Visual Card

Open Visualization Pane

Select Visual Card

Drag the visual to the dashboard.

Click on table Measure and select Sales per MTh measure drag it into the fields. Select the Sales per MTh   Measure table and change the format to whole number and number of decimals from Auto to "0".
