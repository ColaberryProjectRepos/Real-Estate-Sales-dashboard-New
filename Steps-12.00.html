Creating DAX measures and Refresh Date 12


Step 1

 

Create a DAX Measure follow these steps:

1.     Select the table “Measure”

2.     Right Click and select New Measure

3.     Write the following DAX Measures on the space provide one at a time and click the right mark

Sales Per MTh = CALCULATE (SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),

FILTER ('Real_Estate_Sales', MONTH('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date])))

Sales MOM% =IF(ISFILTERED('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date]),

ERROR ("Time intelligence quick measures can only be

 Grouped or filtered by the Power BI-provided date hierarchy or

Primary date column."),

DIVIDE (

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]) - CALCULATE(

 

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),

DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date].[Date], -1, MONTH)),

CALCULATE (

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),

DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date].[Date], -1, MONTH))))

Sales = CALCULATE (SUM('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date Recorded],-1, MONTH))

Sales MOM = 'Measure'[total sales]-'Measure'[saleslm]

 

 

Sales YOY% =IF(

ISFILTERED('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date]),

ERROR ("Time intelligence quick measures can

Only be grouped or filtered by

 The Power BI-provided date hierarchy or primary date column."),

DIVIDE (

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]) - CALCULATE (

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),

DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date].[Date], -1, YEAR)),

CALCULATE (

SUM ('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]),

DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date].[Date], -1, YEAR))))

SalesLy = CALCULATE (SUM('Real_Estate_Sales'[Sale Amount]), DATEADD('Real_Estate_Sales'[Date Recorded], -1,YEAR))

Sales YOY = 'Measure'[total sales]-'Measure'[salesly]

TATOL Assessed value = SUM('Real_Estate_Sales'[Assessed Value])

Total sales = SUM(Real_Estate_Sales[Sale Amount])

 

Property tax = IF('Measure'[TATOL Assessed value]>=3000000,

'Measure'[TATOL Assessed value]*0.19,

'Measure'[TATOL Assessed value]*0.15)

 

Property Tax% = DIVIDE ('Real_Estate_Sales'[property tax],'Real_Estate_Sales'[Total sales])

 

YOY% arrow indicators = SWITCH (TRUE (),

'Measure'[Sales YOY%] <=0, UNICHAR (11167),

'Measure'[Sales YOY%] > 0.01, UNICHAR (11165)," "

 

)

MOM% arrow indicators = SWITCH (TRUE (),

'Measure'[Sales MOM%] <=- 0.001, UNICHAR (11167),

'Measure'[Sales MOM%]> 0.001, UNICHAR (11165)," "

  )





Refreshed Date

Steps

1.    Ø Click on Transform Data found under home tab.

2.    Click on New Source – Blank query

3.    On the space provided write =DateTime.LocalNow() & hit the check mark

4.    Then we gave a name (Data Refresh) for our table under the properties

5.    Click “To Table” found under File & rename the column to “Date Time”

6.    Duplicate the actual column and rename it “Date”. After that go to “Transform” and under date click “Date Only”

7.    Duplicate the actual column and rename it “Time”. Then go to transform and under Time click “Time Only”

8.    Close and Apply
